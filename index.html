About

ArnavCodes Club is a free student-led coding club focused on teaching programming, building projects, and hosting hackathons for students worldwide.

About Me

Hi, I'm Arnav, a student developer with a passion for technology, programming, and learning how things work. I enjoy exploring areas such as Python, web development, artificial intelligence, and hardware projects, always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow. I created ArnavCodes Club as a place to share what I learn, document my projects, and make knowledge more accessible to others. I believe that learning is most valuable when it is shared, and this website serves as both a record of my journey and a resource for anyone interested in technology. Whether I'm building a new project, experimenting with an idea, or learning a new skill, my goal is to keep improving, stay curious, and help others learn along the way.
